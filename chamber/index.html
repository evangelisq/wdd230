Langley Chamber of Commerce

CONNECTING, SUPPORTING, AND ADVOCATING FOR LANGLEY BUSINESS.

The Greater Langley Chamber of Commerce is one of the largest and most active business association in the region, representing over 1,100 member businesses. Established in 1931, it has been a cornerstone for supporting connecting, and advocating for the Langley business community for over 90 years. The Chamber provides resources, networking oppotunities, and advocacy efforts to help business thrive. It also offers to help busnesses thrive. It also offers programs, events, and initiatives to foster collaborationand growth within the community.
